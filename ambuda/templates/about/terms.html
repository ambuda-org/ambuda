Terms of Use

Ambuda's mission is to make the Sanskrit tradition radically accessible by creating a complete archive of traditional Sanskrit literature, and allowing others to collaborate on proofing these texts.

You are free to read and share texts on Ambuda, as well as contribute to adding and transcribing texts through proofing, under the following conditions:

  • You are legally responsible for any content you contribute to Ambuda.
  • Be kind and respectful to others; do not harass, abuse, or violate the privacy of others.
  • Do not violate the law, including appropriate copyright laws.
  • Do not engage in activity that can harm our technical infrastructure or disrupt the ability of other users to use Ambuda.

If you have any questions about these terms, please contact us.
