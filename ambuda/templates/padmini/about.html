About Padmini

Padmini is a smart Sanskrit dictionary that understands Sanskrit words and sentences. Padmini is available both as a standalone tool and as part of Ambuda's digital library of Sanskrit literature.

Most of Padmini's dictionary data comes from other projects, and you can find a full list of those projects here. We are deeply grateful to these projects and their work for making Padmini possible.

Otherwise, Padmini relies heavily on our Vidyut toolkit, which provides reliable infrastructure for Sanskrit software. Although we created Vidyut for Ambuda's specific needs, we hope that Vidyut can help provide the digital foundation for Sanskrit software of all kinds.

Long-term, we hope that Padmini can thoughtfully explain any Sanskrit expression. Here are some directions we are excited about:

  • English-Sanskrit lookup (and likewise for languages other than English)
  • Parses of sentences and compounds
  • Creating anvayas (prose reorderings) of Sanskrit verses
  • Corpus search across our library
  • Contextual grammar explanations
  • Text-to-speech support
  • Fuzzy search
  • Metrical recognition

If you would like to contribute on our work on Padmini, Ambuda, or Vidyut, please contact us. If you are interested in contributing technically, please see the CONTRIBUTING.md file in our Ambuda repo.
